AML Risk and Compliance Analysts

AML Risk and Compliance Analysts

Our AML Risk and Compliance Analysts are specialized professionals who play a critical role in safeguarding organizations against financial crimes such as money laundering, terrorist financing, and other illicit activities. Here’s how we can support and enhance your organization:

AML Program Development

Design and implement comprehensive AML programs tailored to your organization's needs, ensuring adherence to regulatory requirements.

Transaction Monitoring

Analyze financial transactions to detect and investigate suspicious activities using sophisticated tools.

Risk Assessment

Conduct risk assessments to identify vulnerabilities and ensure appropriate controls are in place.

Regulatory Compliance

Ensure compliance with AML regulations, including BSA, USA PATRIOT Act, and FATF guidelines.

Customer Due Diligence

Verify client identities, assess risk profiles, and monitor transactions for unusual activity.

Enhanced Due Diligence

Conduct in-depth investigations on high-risk clients, including PEPs and high-net-worth individuals.

Suspicious Activity Reporting

Prepare and file Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs) with regulatory authorities as required.

Policy and Procedure Development

Develop and update AML policies and procedures to comply with regulatory changes.

Investigation and Analysis

Investigate suspected money laundering activities, reviewing transactions and financial documents.

Regulatory Reporting

Manage regulatory reports, including Currency Transaction Reports (CTRs) and SARs.

Data Management & Reporting

Use analytics tools to monitor, analyze, and report on AML trends and performance.

Fraud Prevention

Develop strategies to prevent fraud and financial crimes through risk analysis.

Risk Mitigation Strategies

Identify and implement risk mitigation strategies to secure financial operations.

Internal Controls Enhancement

Strengthen AML-related internal controls to improve security and compliance.

Documentation & Record-Keeping

Maintain accurate records of AML-related activities for audits and regulatory reviews.

Training & Development

Provide ongoing AML training to internal teams to ensure compliance and awareness.


By leveraging the expertise of our AML Risk and Compliance Analysts, your organization can effectively manage and mitigate risks, ensure regulatory compliance, and protect against financial crime, ultimately safeguarding its reputation and operational integrity.
